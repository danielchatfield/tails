[[!meta title="Talking in public with other Tails developers and users"]]

If you want to talk in public with other Tails developers and users:

  • you may want to join our [[chatroom|chat]]; note: a rather small subset of Tails core developers hang out there, so asynchronous communication means are to be preferred for proposals, volunteering and anything else that might be of some interest for the whole Tails development community;
  • the [[forum]] is open for discussions that do not belong to bugs, feature requests and the like;
  • you can subscribe or write to the tails-dev@boum.org mailing-list; any message sent to this list is stored in a public archive, so beware of what your email content and headers reveal about yourself: location, IP address, etc.

For matters that need to be hidden from the public eyes, you can send email to the private development mailing list: tails@boum.org; encrypting such email with our [[GnuPG key]] is the only way to achieve end-to-end encryption.
