If you want to talk to Tails developers or users:
  • you may want to join our [[chatroom|chat]];
  • the [[forum]] is open for discussions that do not belong to bugs, feature requests and the like;
  • you can send email to the private development mailing list: amnesia@boum.org; encrypting such email with our [[GnuPG key]] is the only way to achieve end-to-end encryption.
