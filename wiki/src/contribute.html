[[!meta title="Contribute"]]

There are many ways to contribute to T(A)ILS: you can write specifications or documentation, describe your usecases, translate this website into your native language, [[setup a mirror]]. Read on!

Tools

This wiki is used as a basic bug tracking system:

  • [[/bugs]]
  • [[/TODO]]
  • [[/wishlist]]
  • [[patches|/patch]]
The tags assigned to these TODO items (e.g. todo/translate, todo/research) will hopefully help you find a way to participate.

Building images

Before contributing your changes back, You probably want to test your changes before contributing them back; this may require you to [[build an ISO image|build]]. This step is also needed if you need to [[customize]] T(A)ILS.
